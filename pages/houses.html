Hogwarts Houses


Gryffindor

Bravery. Courage. Daring.

Home to the bold and the fearless, Gryffindors stand up for what is right — no matter the cost.


Slytherin

Ambition. Cunning. Determination.

Slytherins are strategic, resourceful, and unafraid to chase greatness — even in unconventional ways.


Ravenclaw

Wisdom. Wit. Curiosity.

Ravenclaws value knowledge, creativity, and original thinking. For them, learning never ends.


Hufflepuff

Loyalty. Patience. Hard work.

Hufflepuffs are kind, fair, and dependable — the heart of Hogwarts, often underestimated but never outmatched.
