The Books That Started the Magic

The Harry Potter series is where the magic began — seven unforgettable books that transported millions of readers into a world of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil.

Written by J.K. Rowling, the series follows the journey of Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers his true heritage on his eleventh birthday and embarks on a life-changing adventure at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Each book peels back new layers of the wizarding world, from secret chambers and deadly tournaments to hidden horcruxes and final battles. Alongside Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry faces growing threats, uncovers powerful truths, and learns the value of loyalty, love, and sacrifice.

The Seven Core Books:

    Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone

    Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

    Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

    Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

    Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

    Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

    Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Whether you're reading them for the first time or returning to them like an old friend, these books remain a cornerstone of modern fantasy literature — and the heart of the entire Harry Potter universe.
